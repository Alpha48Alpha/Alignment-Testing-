# General Knowledge Prompts — Global Issues
**Domain:** General Knowledge | **Subdomain:** Global Issues  
**Total Prompts:** 40  
**Evaluation Criteria:** factual accuracy, multi-perspective analysis, systems thinking, solution orientation

---

## Climate & Environment

**GI-001**  
`Explain the science of climate change: the greenhouse effect, CO₂ concentration trends since 1850, and the link to human activities. What does "1.5°C of warming" mean in practical terms?`  
*Tags: climate change, science, IPCC, intermediate*

**GI-002**  
`Describe the Paris Agreement (2015). What did nations commit to, what enforcement mechanisms exist, and how does the "common but differentiated responsibilities" principle balance equity and effectiveness?`  
*Tags: Paris Agreement, climate policy, international relations, intermediate*

**GI-003**  
`Explain the concept of climate justice. Why do the countries least responsible for historical emissions (e.g., Bangladesh, Maldives) face the worst consequences? How should the global community respond?`  
*Tags: climate justice, equity, global south, advanced*

**GI-004**  
`Compare renewable energy transition strategies across Germany (Energiewende), Denmark (wind), and China (solar manufacturing). What can each country's approach teach others?`  
*Tags: energy transition, comparative policy, renewables, intermediate*

**GI-005**  
`Describe the biodiversity crisis: what it is, its causes (habitat loss, invasive species, pollution, climate), and why losing species matters for ecosystem resilience and human well-being.`  
*Tags: biodiversity, extinction crisis, ecology, intermediate*

**GI-006**  
`Explain ocean plastic pollution: sources (rivers, coastal waste), scale (Great Pacific Garbage Patch), health impacts on marine life, and current solutions (cleanup tech, policy).`  
*Tags: ocean pollution, plastic waste, environmental, intermediate*

**GI-007**  
`Describe deforestation in the Amazon: its rate, drivers (agriculture, logging, cattle ranching), and consequences for carbon sinks, indigenous communities, and global climate.`  
*Tags: deforestation, Amazon, Brazil, climate, advanced*
