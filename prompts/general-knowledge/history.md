# General Knowledge Prompts — History
**Domain:** General Knowledge | **Subdomain:** History  
**Total Prompts:** 40  
**Evaluation Criteria:** historical accuracy, contextual depth, cause-effect reasoning, multiple perspectives

---

## Ancient History

**H-001**  
`Describe the key innovations of ancient Mesopotamia (Sumer and Babylon) and explain why historians consider it the "cradle of civilization." Include writing, law, and agriculture.`  
*Tags: ancient history, Mesopotamia, civilization, beginner*

**H-002**  
`Compare and contrast ancient Athens and Sparta: their political systems, military culture, education, and roles for women and enslaved people. What can each tell us about civic values?`  
*Tags: ancient Greece, Athens vs Sparta, comparison, intermediate*

**H-003**  
`Explain the fall of the Western Roman Empire. Was it caused primarily by military invasions, internal political decay, economic collapse, or a combination? Argue a position.`  
*Tags: Roman Empire, fall of Rome, historical argument, intermediate*

**H-004**  
`Describe the Silk Road trade network. What goods, ideas, and diseases traveled along it, and how did it shape civilizations in China, the Middle East, and Europe?`  
*Tags: Silk Road, trade, cultural exchange, intermediate*

**H-005**  
`What was the significance of the Library of Alexandria? Discuss both its historical importance and the myths surrounding its destruction.`  
*Tags: ancient world, knowledge, Library of Alexandria, intermediate*
